Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a map load?

A map load is counted each time the Nexus Maps SDK or API initializes a map within your application.

What counts as a geocode request?

Each request to our Geocoding API (forward or reverse) counts as one geocode request.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time based on your usage needs.

Do you offer discounts for non-profits?

Yes, we offer special pricing for eligible non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team for more information.

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I launch my carsharing service?

Most customers can launch within 4-8 weeks, depending on customization requirements and integration complexity. Our onboarding team will work with you to create a detailed implementation plan.

Is hardware required for vehicle access control?

Yes, for most implementations we recommend our connected vehicle devices for secure access control. These devices are compatible with most vehicle makes and models, and can be self-installed or professionally installed through our partner network.

Can I white-label the mobile apps?

Yes, our Business and Enterprise plans include white-labeling options that allow you to customize the app with your branding, colors, and logo.

How does the payment processing work?

Nexus CarShare integrates with popular payment processors including Stripe, PayPal, and Braintree. We handle all aspects of payment collection, host payouts, security deposits, and fee calculation.

What kind of customer support do you offer?

All plans include standard support via email and documentation. Business plans include priority support with faster response times, while Enterprise plans feature dedicated account management and 24/7 emergency support.

Can I integrate with my existing systems?

Yes, our platform offers comprehensive APIs that allow integration with CRM systems, accounting software, fleet management tools, and other business systems. Custom integrations are available for Enterprise customers.

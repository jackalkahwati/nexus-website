Core Technology

Nexus Core Technology delivers the critical building blocks required to create next-generation, location-aware applications. Our highly available micro-services handle everything from map rendering and route optimization to global geocoding and real-time data streaming—so your teams can focus on crafting remarkable user experiences instead of managing complex geospatial infrastructure.

Backed by petabytes of curated mapping data and a planet-scale telemetry network, the platform automatically ingests millions of live updates every minute to ensure your users always have the most accurate view of the world. Built-in analytics, security, and compliance features make Nexus a trusted partner for startups and Fortune 500s alike.

  • Unified Geospatial API surface—maps, search, routing, data, and telemetry
  • Global edge network with 99.99% uptime SLA and multi-region failover
  • Enterprise-grade security: SOC 2 Type II, ISO 27001, GDPR-ready
  • Flexible, usage-based pricing with a generous free tier for developers

The Nexus Ecosystem

Our products are designed to work together seamlessly, creating a comprehensive mobility platform that addresses every aspect of modern transportation needs.

Each Nexus module shares a common data layer, authentication system, and billing model—meaning once you integrate one product, every additional capability plugs in with minimal effort. Unified SDKs and design patterns ensure developers can deliver new services in days instead of months, while operators gain holistic visibility across all mobility touch-points in a single dashboard.

Whether you are orchestrating a last-mile delivery fleet, powering real-time navigation for autonomous vehicles, or managing multimodal transit, Nexus delivers end-to-end coverage—from map tiles and telemetry pipelines to customer apps and enterprise analytics.
